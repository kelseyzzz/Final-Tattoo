Welcome

We’d like to thank you for checking out our website and would like to give you a quick history of our shop. Both of us had spent several years working under some “old timers” in Florida learning the history and what a good tattoo shop should be. Eventually we settled down in Atlanta, Shay’s hometown. Feeling it was time to open a shop that is up to our standards, we put together our ideas and came up with a layout that is comfortable, fun, and open but also allows for privacy as needed. We opened Liberty in the summer of 2002 naming it after our daughter born in the spring of the same year. The shop has grown a lot over the years and we’ve been lucky enough to leave our mark on thousands of people along the way.

Shay and Kaki Cannon
